My Birthplace

I was born in Jamshedpur on 5th September 2001. I was born in tata main hospital (i.e. one of the largest hospitals in Jharkhand)

Jamshedpur or Tatanagar is the largest and most populous city in Jharkhand and the first planned industrial city in India. It was ranked as the cleanest city in India in the year 2019. It was founded by Jamsetji Tata, founder of Tata Groups in the 1900s, and was named after him. It was established in 1919. Jamshedpur was ranked as the 18th cleanest city of India in 2022 by Swach Survekshan 2022. Jamshedpur was ranked the 7th cleanest city of India in 2010. The city is also ranked as 2nd in India in terms of 'Quality of Life. Jamshedpur is the 84th fastest growing city in the world according to City Mayors Foundation.
